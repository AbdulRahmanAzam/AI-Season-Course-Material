ACME Online Store — Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Eligibility
Customers may request a refund within 30 days of purchase for most physical and digital products.
Items must be unused and in original packaging where applicable.

2. Non-refundable items
Gift cards, personalized products, and clearance items marked "final sale" are not eligible for refunds.

3. How to request a refund
Email support@acme-store.example with your order number and reason for the refund.
Our team responds within 2 business days.

4. Refund processing time
Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.
Bank processing times may add extra days.

5. Partial refunds
If only part of an order is returned, you receive a partial refund for the returned items only.
Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

6. Contact
For questions about this policy, contact support@acme-store.example or call 1-800-555-0199.
