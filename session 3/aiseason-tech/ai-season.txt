AI Season is a live online AI Agents bootcamp also known as an agentic AI bootcamp. It is designed for Pakistani students, early-career developers, and beginners who want to build real AI agents instead of only watching tutorials.
AI Season is also written as AISeason. The program focuses on practical AI engineering, agentic AI systems, LangChain, LangGraph, Retrieval Augmented Generation, tool calling, memory, evaluation, observability, and shipping AI agents to production.
The AI Season bootcamp is a 6-week program with 18 live online sessions. The first summer 2026 cohort starts on 1st July 2026 and runs until 12th August 2026.
AI Season is taught online. Students can attend the live classes remotely. The sessions are taught in Urdu and English so that Pakistani students can understand concepts clearly while still learning technical terms used in the AI industry.
The bootcamp was created by Abdul Rahman Azam. Abdul Rahman Azam is the founder and lead instructor of AI Season. He is also described as a FAST NUCES alumnus.
AI Season is beginner-friendly. Basic Python knowledge is recommended, but the AI Agents part is taught from scratch. The goal is to help learners move from basic Python to building real agentic AI systems.
The early-bird fee for AI Season is PKR 2,250. The original price is PKR 3,000. The early-bird price is described as Pakistan-focused pricing, and international students can pay the same price.
The top three ranked students of every AI Season cohort receive a full refund. This refund challenge is meant to reward serious students who perform strongly during the cohort.
AI Season teaches AI Agents fundamentals including planning, tool use, and memory. It also teaches LangChain, LangGraph, RAG, agent evaluation, observability, and production deployment concepts.
